Nachos

Desription

How to make a delicious baked nachos. Honestly, it is the best.

Ingredients

  • Corn Chips
  • 2 cans of beans
  • 1 jar of past suace
  • Cayan Pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Tasty cheese
  • Greek yoghurt

Steps

  1. Add the beans and pasta sauce into a medium mixing bowl
  2. Crumb the feta and add to the mixing bowl
  3. Put 10 shakes of cayan pepper into the mixing bowl
  4. Mix the ingredients together
  5. Place a handful of cornchips into 2 small baking dishes
  6. Split the bean mix evenly between the 2 baking dishes
  7. Put some grated cheese ontop and cook at 180C for 20 minutes
  8. When ready, serve with 1 table spoon of greek yoghurt
